## What is Pynqo?

Pynqo is a powerful Discord monitoring solution that helps you stay on top of conversations that matter to you. Define your personal keywords and receive instant notifications when they appear in Discord channels, ensuring you never miss topics you care about.

<Info>
Perfect for staying updated on your interests, tracking mentions of your projects, or following specific discussions across Discord communities.
</Info>

## How does Pynqo work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Your Keywords">
    Define the topics, names, or phrases you want to follow across Discord.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Channels">
    Select which Discord channels you want Pynqo to monitor for your keywords.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Notified">
    Receive instant alerts whenever your keywords appear in conversations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Pynqo works in the background across your Discord servers, continuously monitoring for your personal keywords without affecting your Discord experience.
</Tip>
