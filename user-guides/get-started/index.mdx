# Welcome to Pynqo

Pynqo helps you track conversations that matter. Define your keywords and get instant notifications when they're mentioned in Discord channels.

<Info>
Stay updated on your interests, projects, or discussions across Discord—automatically.
</Info>

## How Pynqo Works

<Diagram>
  {/* Diagram: User sets keywords → Pynqo monitors channels → User receives notification */}
</Diagram>

1. **Set Your Keywords**: Define topics, names, or phrases to follow.
2. **Choose Channels**: Select which Discord channels to monitor.
3. **Get Notified**: Receive alerts instantly when your keywords appear.

<Tip>
Pynqo works in the background—no manual monitoring needed.
</Tip>
